Bentota Turtle Hatchery

The Bentota Turtle Hatchery is located on the southern coast of Sri Lanka and is a popular destination for tourists who want to learn about sea turtles and support conservation efforts. The hatchery was established in 1986 and is run by a local community organization.

Activities at Bentota Turtle Hatchery

  • Turtle hatchery tour
  • Turtle feeding
  • Turtle release
  • Educational programs

Conservation Efforts

The Bentota Turtle Hatchery works to protect sea turtle populations in several ways, including:

  • Collecting and incubating turtle eggs
  • Releasing hatchlings into the ocean
  • Providing medical care to injured turtles
  • Researching sea turtle behavior and ecology
