Turtle Hatcheries in Sri Lanka

Welcome to our website showcasing the turtle hatcheries in Sri Lanka. Our mission is to protect and conserve the turtle population in Sri Lanka.

Turtle Categories

Sri Lanka is home to five species of sea turtles:

  • Green Turtle: most common turtle found in Sri Lanka's waters.
  • Hawksbill Turtle: Known for their beautiful shells and found in coral reefs.
  • Olive Ridley Turtle: The smallest of the sea turtles and known for their synchronized nesting.
  • Loggerhead Turtle: Known for their large head and powerful jaws, they are rare in Sri Lanka.
  • Leatherback Turtle: The largest of the sea turtles, they are rare in Sri Lanka.

Each species has its own unique characteristics and habitat, and all of them are endangered due to human activities. We must take action to protect these wonderful creatures!
